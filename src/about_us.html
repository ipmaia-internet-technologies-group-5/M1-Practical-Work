About Us

Who we are?

We believe that sustainable forests are critical to our collective future. Save the forest is a sustainability leader through our work in standards, conservation, community, and education.

As an independent, non-profit organization, we collaborate with our diverse network to provide solutions to local and global sustainability challenges. Save the forest works with the forest sector, conservation groups, resource professionals.

How do we work

We collaborate, restore, improve livelihoods, and measure our impact.

Our vision

A world where communities and nature sustainably thrive together to stop global warming in our lifetime.

Our values

The values that inspire our work are: Integrity, Transparency, Excellence, Collaboration, Passion and Courage.

Our goal

Our goal is to reach over 150 million trees – equivalent to around 127 500 hectares – restored or conserved by the end of 2030.

Our Staff

SaveTheForest Alliance's talented, diverse and diverse team works around the world, side by side with farmers, forest communities, businesses, civil society organizations, governments and millions of individuals to enable positive change along global supply chains across many of our most important natural landscapes.
